About the project

Acting as an RPA Consultant, this national project came with the aim of further materializing the digital transformation through process automation in one of the largest cosmetics companies in Latin America, called Grupo Boticário. As it is a much more integrated company with different technologies, Boticário constantly looked for ways to optimize each of its processes, through constant continuous improvements, ensuring that the least possible effort is expended on excessively time-consuming activities. Working directly with the former GAVB group, my objective was to bring practical solutions even closer to obtaining and saving time, as well as identifying possible inputs/data that could be used on numerous other fronts of the company.

Project goals

Digital transformation

The Boticário Group, precisely because of its expressiveness in Latin America, is constantly seeking Integrate technology even further into your daily life, bringing even more security to any and all activities necessary for the established performance flow.

Hour savings

Another objective is due to the interest of starting to save hours spent on massive and time-consuming activities, where this would allow the company to redirect its efforts to other fronts of business, seeking to further expand its annual revenue.

Marketing Positioning

Outro objetivo é por conta do interesse de iniciar uma economia de horas desprendidas em atividades massantes e delongadas, onde isso permitiria com que a empresa pudesse redirecionar seus esforços para outras frentes de negócio, buscando ampliar ainda mais o seu faturamento anual.

Project impacts

The speed at which we were able to introduce process automation within the company was remarkable. Solutions were even created where robots produced "pre-ready robots" to further speed up the process development and delivery of proposed demands. The result of this was a huge saving of time in countless activities, as well as the stability and resilience of robots built so that the business area can not only benefit from of the precision proposed by a robot, but also of the time savings that it could offer you
